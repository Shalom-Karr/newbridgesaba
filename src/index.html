The Journey to Stability

Getting started is simple, transparent, and fast.

01

Intake

We begin by listening to your story and answering your questions to ensure you feel confident and guided from day one.

02

Insurance

Our team verifies your insurance and authorizations to ensure your child receives the maximum support and services they are entitled to.

03

Assessment

A kind, experienced BCBA will spend time with your child in a comfortable, play-based setting. This thorough evaluation helps us see where they are today and, more importantly, where they have the potential to go.

04

Implementation & Review

Once we’ve designed a plan that feels right for your family, we begin services in a consistent, professional, and timely manner. Through weekly and monthly reviews, we stay in constant communication with you to ensure the plan is yielding real-world results and evolving as your child grows.
