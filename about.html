Our Story & Mission

Who We
Are.

New Bridges ABA is a dedicated team of clinical professionals committed to providing consistent, high-quality care for children with Autism Spectrum Disorder across New Jersey and Ohio.

Dedicated to Your Child's Success.

At New Bridges ABA, we view each child as an individual with unique strengths and potential. Our mission is to provide the "bridge" between different environments—school, home, and community—ensuring that progress is never interrupted.

We are more than just service providers; we are partners in your child's journey. Our empathetic BCBAs and skilled RBTs work tirelessly to create customized interventions that empower children to communicate, interact, and thrive.

Our Services

What We Offer.

🏠

In-Home Therapy

We bring evidence-based ABA therapy to the comfort of your home, focusing on daily living skills and family integration.

🏫

School Support

Our therapists accompany your child to school, providing 1:1 behavioral coaching and social skills training in the classroom.

👪

Parent Training

We empower parents with the tools and strategies they need to support their child's progress when our therapists aren't present.
