Consistent School Support

ABA services in school are an effective way to help children with ASD progress at their own pace within a structured setting. Our therapists attend school with your child to provide:

  • ✔
    1:1 Behavior Intervention: Real-time redirection and support during classroom activities.
  • ✔
    Social Skills Coaching: Helping your child interact with peers during recess and group work.
  • ✔
    IEP Goal Alignment: Working closely with teachers and school staff in NJ & OH districts.

Predictable In-Home Care

But what happens when they get home? Studies show children benefit greatly from generalization. That’s why our therapists work with your child both in school and at home.

  • ✔
    Skill Generalization: Using school-learned skills in a natural home environment.
  • ✔
    Daily Living Mastery: Independence in eating, dressing, and hygiene.
  • ✔
    Sibling & Family Integration: Helping the whole family unit communicate effectively.

Our Clinical Pillars

We focus on 5 core areas to ensure your child develops into a confident, independent individual.

Communication

Focusing on vocal speech, conversation cues, and social prompts to develop functional language across all contexts.

Behavior Management

Applying consistent reinforcement and intervention to help children understand expectations and consequences.

Daily Living

Reinforcing routines so your child gains independence in tasks like grooming, dressing, and eating.

Cognitive Growth

Developing memory and learning skills, such as following complex instructions and task completion.

Emotional Regulation

Providing tools for self-regulation to help your child manage stress and adapt to challenging new situations.
