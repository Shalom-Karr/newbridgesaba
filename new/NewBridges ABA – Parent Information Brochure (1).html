Frequently Asked
Questions

What exactly is ABA therapy?

Applied Behavior Analysis uses evidence-based strategies to teach new skills, reduce challenging behaviors, and improve quality of life — always tailored to your child's unique needs and personality.

How long will my child need services?

Duration varies by child. Our team continuously reviews progress and adjusts intensity to ensure your child receives exactly what they need at each stage of development.

How involved will I be as a parent?

Deeply involved — parent participation is a cornerstone of our approach. We provide regular training and updates so you feel confident supporting your child every day.

Does my child need a diagnosis to enroll?

A formal autism diagnosis is typically required for insurance coverage. We're happy to help guide your family through the evaluation process.

🛡️ Insurance & Funding

We accept most major insurance plans and handle prior authorizations on your behalf. Medicaid accepted. Private-pay and sliding-scale options also available.
